## Prologue: Falling Skies

> **Quest:** "Falling Skies"  
> **Prerequisite:** Prapor Loyalty Level 2  
> **Location:** Woods (crashed plane)

### Objectives

1. **Locate the fallen plane** - Find the downed plane on Woods
2. **Reach Prapor LL2** - Required for Standard/non-EOD accounts
3. **Ask traders about the plane** - Gather intel
4. **Gather information from Therapist**
   - *Optional:* Pay Therapist $2,000 for faster info
5. **Retrieve flash drive** - From G-Wagon SUV near Shoreline Tunnel
6. **Hand over flash drive to Prapor**
7. **Wait for Prapor** (time gate)
8. **Retrieve the plane's flight recorder**
9. **Leave flight recorder** - Shoreline, fishers' island house corner room
10. **Visit Prapor**
11. **Hand over items:**
    - 2Ã— Toolset
    - 3Ã— Rechargeable battery
    - 5Ã— Printed circuit board
12. **Wait for Prapor** (longer time gate)
13. **Hand over flight crew's transcript**
14. **Hand over Elektronik's secure flash drive**
15. **Wait for Prapor**
16. **Retrieve the armored case** from the plane wreckage

## Prapor's Branch (Hand Over Case)

> If you gave the case to Prapor

### Quest: "The Ticket" (Prapor Path)

1. **Build Intelligence Center Level 1** in Hideout
   - Required to communicate with Mr. Kerman
2. **Wait for Mr. Kerman's call**
3. **Contact Kerman** via hideout intel center
4. **Investigate Lighthouse Camp**
   - Follow Kerman's lead to Prapor's camp on Lighthouse
   - Search for clues about Prapor's intentions
5. **Unlock Lightkeeper**
   - Complete required tasks to gain access
   - Speak with Lightkeeper
6. **Recover the Case**
   - Lightkeeper returns the armored case contents after you fulfill his requests

> **At this point, both paths converge** - you now possess the closed case and must open it.

---

## Independent Branch (Keep Case)

> If you kept the case for yourself

### Quest: "The Ticket" (Keep Path)

1. **Build Intelligence Center Level 1** in Hideout
2. **Mr. Kerman contacts you** once comms are online
3. **Ask Mechanic for help**
   - Case is electronically locked
   - Mechanic suggests signal jammer from TerraGroup Lab
4. **Prepare for Lab**
   - Acquire Labs keycard if needed
5. **Obtain Signal Jammer** from The Lab
6. **Unlock the Case** using the jammer
   - Inside: "Ticket" keycard + encrypted instructions
7. **Read Instructions** - Contains original escape plan
8. **Report to Kerman** - Inform him of contents

## Go Your Own Way (Refuse Kerman)

> If you refused to trust Mr. Kerman

### Steps

1. **Head to Terminal**
   - Case's plan directs you to port Terminal
   - Move through Shoreline to reach entrance checkpoint
2. **Use the Ticket Keycard**
   - Swipe at Terminal gate's intercom reader
   - Alerts authorities/extraction controllers
3. **Meet Prapor**
   - Prapor arrives with his men
   - He controls this exit

### Prapor's Demand

Prapor insists you pay **â‚½500,000,000** to use Terminal and leave Tarkov safely.
